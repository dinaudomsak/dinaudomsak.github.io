About

A Leader Shaped by Two Worlds

With over 15 years of experience at the intersection of higher education and corporate learning, Dr. Dina Udomsak brings a rare dual fluency to every engagement — the rigor of academia and the results-orientation of the Fortune 500.

Her work spans higher education administration, where she has shaped institutional strategy and student success, and Learning & Development leadership within global supply chain organizations, where she has built learning ecosystems that move the needle on performance.

Dr. Udomsak believes that great learning is both art and science — grounded in research, alive in practice, and always in service of human potential.
